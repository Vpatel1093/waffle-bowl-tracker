What is the Waffle Bowl?

The Waffle Bowl is a losers bracket where the bottom 6 teams in the league compete in the playoffs. The twist? You want to LOSE. The ultimate loser of the Waffle Bowl earns the dubious honor of last place in the league.

How It Works

Bracket Structure (6 Teams)

  • Seeding: Bottom 6 teams by record enter the bracket
  • The Twist: Seeds 1-2 (worst two teams) get BYES in Round 1
  • Week 1 (Quarterfinals): Seeds 3v6 and 4v5 compete
  • Week 2 (Semifinals): QF losers play the bye teams
  • Week 3 (Finals): Semifinal losers compete for LAST PLACE ðŸ§‡
